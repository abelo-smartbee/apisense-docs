# Protocol 4 — dead bees from the bottom board

Collect a sample of dead bees from the hive's bottom board for laboratory testing.

## Materials

- **Paper envelope** (one per colony)
- Disposable gloves

!!! warning "Paper envelope only"
    Do not use bubble mailers, plastic bags, foil pouches, or glass jars — in those containers **the sample will get mouldy**.

## Procedure

1. **Gently mix** the dead bees on the bottom board.
2. Collect **~½ cup** of dead bees (minimum 30 individuals).

    If there are **fewer than 30 dead bees** on the board — collect **all of them**.

3. Rule: **1 colony = 1 sample**.
4. Transfer the sample into a **paper envelope**.

!!! tip "Can't ship immediately?"
    The sample can be stored **in a freezer** until shipping.

## Register the sample in the app

1. In the Apisense app: ***add → register sample → collection date → sample type = dead bees***.
2. The app generates a **unique test code**.
3. **Write the code on the envelope** with a permanent marker.

## Shipping

Add this sample to the package described in [Shipping samples](index.md#shipping-samples).
