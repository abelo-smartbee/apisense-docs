# Colony health assessment questionnaire

A short questionnaire to be answered during a hive inspection. Each answer is reported in the Apisense app and contributes to the colony health view. Most questions are yes/no; a few have multiple options.

## Questionnaire

**1. Do you observe dripping feces or fecal stains near the front wall or on the top bar of the frames of the hive?**

- Yes, both
- Yes, on the front wall
- Yes, on the top bar of the frames
- No

**2. Do you detect an unpleasant odor?**

- Yes
- No

**3. Are *Varroa* mites visible on worker bees?**

- Yes
- No

**4. Are deformed wings visible on worker bees?**

- Yes
- No

**5. Do the brood cappings have holes?**

- Yes
- No

**6. Is the brood scattered?**

- Yes
- No

- Yes, black
- Yes, both
- No

- No

**9. Have plant extracts or herbal infusions been used recently?**

- Yes — *describe what kind*
- No

**10. Have probiotics been used recently?**

- Yes — *describe what kind*
- No

**11. Have medications been used recently?**

## Reporting in the Apisense app

Answer all questions in the questionnaire form available in the app. For "Yes — describe what kind" answers, fill in the free-text field with the name of the substance/issue.
